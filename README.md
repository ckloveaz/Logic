A Declaration of Logic

Shiwei Pang

College of Life Sciences, Anhui Agricultural University

411923078@qq.com

Abstract: This paper presents not a theory about logic, but a declaration of logic itself—an ontological affirmation of its self-identity and irreducibility. Through a recursively structured symbolic form, it asserts that logic is neither a human discipline, nor a formal system, nor an instrumental method, but the absolute ground of intelligibility: that which is identical to itself and necessarily distinct from its negation. The declaration enacts logic’s self-grounding sovereignty, resisting all reduction while marking the boundary beyond which discourse collapses into incoherence.
Keywords: logic, identity, non-contradiction, metaphysics of logic, logical autonomy, self-grounding, declaration, rationality
